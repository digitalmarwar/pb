About Me

Prakash Bhakar, 34 years old young Businessman and social worker of Makrana assembly constituency of Nagaur district.Prakash Bhakar was born in a poor farmer’s family in Jusari Gram Panchayat. In 2005, he started his own Makrana marble business. Contested the election of Panchayat Samiti member in 2015, in which won by 137 votes. From here Bhakar started his political and social life. In 2020, he was elected sarpanch of his own gram panchayat Jusari as well as the sarpanch of Nagaur district was elected union president. Bhaker is also a member of Jalore Marble Association.
